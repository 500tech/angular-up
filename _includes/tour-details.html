Itinerary

Make your trip even more memorable, and explore the rare beauty of Israel. Join your fellow Angularists on a magical 1 day trip to Israel’s must-see sights !

Jerusalem

We’ll depart at 8:30AM from Tel Aviv to start our journey with a guided tour through the ancient streets of the Old City of Jerusalem. It is a spiritual center for Judaism, Christianity and Islam - 3 main religions who peacefully co-exist inside those magical city walls. We will visit historic sites such The Church of the Holy Sepulcher, Calvary, The Western Wall and more.

After some shopping time in the famous Jerusalem’s colorful bazaars, our journey will continue south to thee Dead Sea.

The Dead Sea

From Jerusalem we will head all the way down below sea level, to the famous Dead Sea for an afternoon of relaxation. Floating in the magical Dead Sea waters, and covering your body with raw Dead Sea Mud, is known to have both relaxing and healing powers. The Dead Sea is a natural spa, containing over 300 different kinds of minerals, good for your body and your soul.

From there we shall return to Tel Aviv and be back by around 7PM.
